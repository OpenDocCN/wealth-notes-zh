## Chapter 1
### sentences for parents
1. Time to wake up.该起床了。
2. Wake up, you sleepyhead.醒醒，你这个懒鬼。
3. Rise and Shine.太阳照得好高了！
4. Get up,please!Time to go to school.起床了，该去上学了。
5. Still dreaming?还在做梦吗？
6. I'll tickle you if you don't wake up .如果你不醒来我会挠你痒痒的。
7. You should go to bed early to get up early.你应该早睡早起。
8. Want to sleep more?想多睡一会儿吗？
9. Just five more minutes.再睡五分钟。
10. Hurry up,or you'll be late.快点，否则你会迟到。
11. I'll count to three. come on.我数到三，来吧起床。
12. Wow~Did you get up by yourself?哇~你是自己起床的吗？
13. Did you not hear the alarm?你听见闹钟响了吗？
14. Don't doze off.Sit right up.别打瞌睡了，坐起来。
15. Let's see,who gets up first?看看谁先起来了？
16. You still sound asleep.你还在沉睡。
17. Stretch your body.伸展下你的身体。
18. I am not going to wake you up anymore.以后我不会再叫你起床了。
